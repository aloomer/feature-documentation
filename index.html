  • Sending an Appointment Request

    • 1. Navigate to the To-Do Lists Tab
    • 2. Click Fast Fill in the side navigation.
    • 3. Select the date and time of the appointment you wish to fill and click Fill Appointment. A modal window will appear, containing options to edit your message
    • 4. Select your preferred message delivery type.
    • 5. If you wish to manually select recipients, click Choose who receives this message, edit your recipients, and click Continue
    • 6. Edit any template related information and click Send

      • #Note: Your message blast will enter a queued state for 5 minutes, in case you need to cancel the message from sending. After which, your appointment request will be sent.

    • When a patient accepts the appointment time, the appointment detail card will be tagged as "Ready for Booking"

      • #Note: All other message recipients will be notified that the appointment time has been filled.

    • Click View Infoin the corresponding appointment detail card. A modal will appear with the patient information necessary to book the appointment in your practice management software.

  • Viewing ASAP/Short Notice List

    • 1. Navigate to the Reports Tab.
    • 2. Click ASAP List in the side navigation. A list of ASAP or Short Notice contacts will appear: these contacts are synced from your PMS.
